Wolfpack has been created to address critical issues or solve long-standing problems that are hindering nonprofit fundraising work. We possibly exist in the most diverse and dramatic fundraising dancefloor in the world. Solving some of these fundraising problems may lead to significant human progress, driven by the charities. Wolfpack possess over one hundred years of collective nonprofit fundraising experience in Asia, Western Europe and North America. With priceless education from uncountable days and nights of small and significant failures, we take pride in our own integrity standards and wear an unwavering resilient attitude to solve systemic problems in fundraising.

Our goals are- Fundraising ecosystem improvement; advancement of non-profit fundraising by anchoring institutional collaborative projects; and non-profit and development fundraising growth.

Vision

Make people love, admire, respect, and pay for non-profit actions and services that are designed for greater public good.

Mission

Become the fundraising engine for fifty charities in India in the next 5 years. Provide access to reliable and valuable fundraising data to the sector. Produce fifty high value fundraisers for the sector in the next 5 years. Create a credible national platform for non-profits to lean and teach fundraising to each other.
Launch an association of non-profits advancing common causes to policy makers.
